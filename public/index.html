Wedding Day Schedule

Here is an outline of the day to give you an idea of what to expect. This will be updated if there are any changes closer to the time.

14:00 - Wedding Service

This is the wedding ceremony at St Matthew's Church. This will be followed by tea and coffee in the church.

16:00 - Bride and Groom Leave Church

The bride and groom will leave the church to head to the reception venue.

16:30 - Pre-Drinks and Photos at Clare Hall

There will be a drinks reception in Clare Hall with some snacks. During this time, the bride and groom and family will be taking photos in the gardens in Clare Hall.

18:00 - Reception Start

Please be at Clare Hall by 18:00. There will be drinks, food, speeches and dancing!
